About LuvLap Montessori

At LuvLap Montessori House of Children, we follow authentic Montessori principles that help children become confident, independent, and curious learners. Our environment is thoughtfully prepared to support hands-on learning, emotional well-being, and overall development.

Our Facilities

At LuvLap Montessori, we provide a range of facilities to make learning fun, safe, and holistic for every child.

Montessori Work Area
Explore hands-on Montessori activities for creative learning.
Canteen
Healthy meals and snacks to keep little learners energized.
Office
Smooth administration for a safe and organized school environment.
Nature Hub
Connect with nature through our green and outdoor learning space.
Read & Write Corner
Encourage early literacy with cozy reading and writing spaces.
Green Garden
Explore plants, gardening, and hands-on nature learning.
Toddlers Cuddle
A safe and cozy space for little ones to relax and bond.
Sports Zone
Fun physical activities to develop coordination and teamwork.
Kids Park
Play area designed for fun, safety, and social interaction.
Water World
Engage with water play for sensory development and fun.
Clay Play & Sand Land
Develop creativity and motor skills with clay and sand activities.
